Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my Pro subscription? ▾
Open HitScore AI → Go to Profile → Tap "Manage Subscription" → Tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used to purchase. If that doesn't work, email support@gethitscore.com with your order confirmation.
How do I cancel my subscription? ▾
On iPhone: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → HitScore AI → Cancel Subscription. On Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → HitScore AI → Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
Why is my score low even though I think my post is good? ▾
Scores reflect predicted virality based on hook strength, emotional triggers, length, timing, and niche-fit. A good score doesn't mean a bad post — it means the AI sees ways to optimize it. Try using "Fix my post" or the Rephrase tool to see stronger alternatives. Your niche setting also affects scoring, so make sure it's set correctly in Settings.
How do I set my niche? ▾
Go to Settings (bottom nav → Tools → Settings) and tap "Your Niche." You can choose from 11+ niches including Finance, Tech, Fitness, Entertainment, and more. Your niche personalizes every AI response — scores, coaching, trends, and content plans all adapt to your specific audience.
How does the AI work? ▾
HitScore uses Claude (Anthropic's AI) to analyze and score content. Our models are trained on patterns from high-performing social media content across multiple niches. Scores are predictions based on hook strength, controversy potential, audience fit, timing, and trend alignment — not guarantees of virality.
Are trends real-time? ▾
Yes. The Trends screen pulls live data and each trend has a 24-hour expiry. The freshest trend is always ranked #1. You can swipe to delete trends you've already seen, or tap "Use Trend" to instantly start scoring a post based on that topic.
Is my content stored or used to train AI? ▾
Content you submit is processed in real-time to generate your score. We do not sell your content or use it to train AI models. Your account data and scoring history are stored securely. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I delete my account? ▾
Go to Profile → scroll down → tap "Delete Account." You'll see a confirmation warning. Confirming will permanently delete all your data from our servers within 30 days. This action cannot be undone. If you have an active subscription, cancel it separately through Apple or Google before deleting your account.
What platforms does HitScore AI support? ▾
HitScore AI is designed for content creators on X (Twitter), Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Facebook. You can set your primary platform in onboarding or update it in Settings. AI scores, timing recommendations, and rephrasing suggestions all adapt to your selected platform.
